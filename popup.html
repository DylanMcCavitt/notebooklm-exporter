How to export

  1. Open notebooklm.google.com and go to your chat or notes.
  2. Use the floating Export PDF button at the bottom‑right of the page.
  3. Click the button to export the chat specifically.
  4. Math is rendered (KaTeX). Save via Print / Save as PDF or Download PDF.
  5. Additionally, you can highlight a section of text and just export that if you would like.
